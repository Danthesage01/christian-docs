## Getting Started

### Sign Up
1. Open AYA on your phone or browser
2. Tap **Sign Up** and enter your email and password
3. You'll receive a **6-digit verification code** via email — enter it to confirm your account
4. Complete the 4-step profile setup (see below)

### Sign In
- Log in with your email and password, or use **Sign in with Google** for quick access

### Forgot Password
- Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen
- Enter your email to receive a password reset link
- Click the link and set a new password

## Creating Your Profile

The signup wizard walks you through 4 steps:

### Step 1 — Basic Info
- First name and last name
- Date of birth
- Gender

### Step 2 — Faith Profile
- Denomination (e.g., Catholic, Pentecostal, Baptist, Anglican, etc.)
- Church name
- How often you attend church
- Your prayer style
- A life verse (your favourite Bible verse)
- A Bible character you relate to

### Step 3 — About You
- Bio (tell others about yourself)
- What you're searching for: Partner, Prayer Partner, Counselor, or Church Partner
- Lifestyle tags: Sober, Waiting Until Marriage, Family-Oriented, etc.
- City, State, and Country

### Step 4 — Photos
- Upload a profile photo (required)
- Add up to 5 additional gallery photos
- Optionally record a 15-second testimony video

> **Tip:** Complete profiles get more visibility. Fill out every section to increase your chances of matching.

## Discovering Matches

The **Discover** screen shows you one profile at a time in a card stack.

### Swiping
- **Swipe Right** → **Like** — you're interested in this person
- **Swipe Left** → **Pass** — skip to the next profile
- **Swipe Up** → **Super Like** — show strong interest (the other person will know)

### Action Buttons
If you prefer tapping over swiping, use the buttons below the card:
- **Red X** (left) → Pass
- **Gold Star** (center) → Super Like
- **Green Heart** (right) → Like

### Profile Cards
Each card shows:
- Profile photo
- Name and age
- Location (city, state)
- Denomination and church
- Bio and life verse (tap the info icon to expand)

### How Matching Works
- When you **Like** or **Super Like** someone and they do the same for you, it's a **match**
- You'll both get a notification and a conversation is automatically created
- If you **Pass** on someone, they won't appear in your deck again

## Matches & Connections

The **Matches** screen has two tabs:

### Matches Tab
- Shows all your mutual matches (both of you liked each other)
- Displays the date you matched
- Tap on a match to open their profile or start a conversation

### Likes Tab
- Shows people who have liked you but you haven't swiped on yet
- Swipe right on their profile to match, or left to pass

## Messaging

Once you match with someone, you can start chatting.

### Starting a Conversation
- Go to the **Chat** tab or tap the message button on a match
- Type your message and send

### Chat Features
- **Real-time messaging** — messages appear instantly
- **Read receipts** — see when your message has been read
- **Typing indicators** — see when the other person is typing
- **Message history** — scroll up to see previous messages

### Notifications
- You'll receive notifications for new messages even when the app is in the background
- Unread message counts appear on the Chat tab icon

## Talking Stages

AYA tracks the progression of your conversations through three faith-based stages:

### 1. Temple Stage (1–3 weeks)
- The getting-to-know-you phase
- Share your faith journey, values, and what you're looking for
- Daily devotional questions are available as icebreakers

### 2. Courtship Stage (1–3 months)
- Deeper conversations and intentional connection
- You've established mutual interest and compatibility

### 3. Committed Stage (3+ months)
- A committed, faith-centred relationship
- Access to counseling features for couples

> Stages progress naturally based on the length of your conversation.

## Faith Milestones

Track your faith journey and earn badges along the way.

### Sunday Check-in
- Check in at church on Sundays to build your attendance streak
- Uses your phone's location to verify you're at a place of worship
- Upload a photo from the service
- Build weekly streaks and earn badges

### Celibacy Tracker
- A private commitment tracker for abstinence
- Start your streak and check in daily
- Track your current streak and longest streak
- Reset anytime — this is a personal journey
- Completely private — only you can see your tracker

### Mission & Volunteer Work
- Log your mission trips and volunteer experiences
- Upload photos (up to 10 per entry)
- Categorise by type (mission trip, local volunteering, outreach, etc.)
- Share your service journey on your profile

### Achievements & Badges
Earn badges for milestones like:
- **First Match** — matched with someone for the first time
- **Sunday Streak** — consistent church attendance (7, 30, 90 days)
- **Celibacy Milestones** — personal commitment achievements
- **Profile Complete** — filled out every section of your profile
- **Mission Logger** — uploaded mission/volunteer experiences

## Search & Filters

Use the **Search** screen to find specific types of people:

### Available Filters
- **Denomination** — filter by one or more Christian denominations
- **Gender** — who you're looking for
- **Age Range** — set minimum and maximum age
- **Location** — filter by city or state
- **Searching For** — Partner, Prayer Partner, Counselor, or Church Partner

### Special Search Modes
- **Prayer Partner Finder** — find someone to pray with
- **Church Partner Finder** — find someone who attends church in your city

## Counseling

AYA offers a built-in counseling feature for couples who want faith-based guidance.

### How It Works
1. From a conversation, request a counseling session
2. Your request is sent with a status of **Pending**
3. Once a counselor accepts, the session becomes **Active**
4. Schedule an appointment at a time that works for both of you
5. After the session, the counselor may add notes for your reference
6. Sessions can be marked as **Completed** or **Cancelled**

## Safety & Moderation

AYA is committed to keeping the community safe and faith-focused.

### Content Moderation
- Messages are scanned for inappropriate content
- Personal information (emails, phone numbers) is flagged to protect your privacy
- Faith-specific trolling and disrespectful language is detected and blocked

### Reporting a User
If someone makes you uncomfortable:
1. Go to their profile or conversation
2. Tap the **Report** button
3. Select a category:
   - **Safety & Legal** — threats, harassment, illegal activity
   - **Integrity** — fake profile, scam, misleading info
   - **Faith-Specific** — mocking faith, inappropriate religious content
4. Add details and submit
5. The moderation team will review and take action

### Account Actions
- Users who violate community guidelines may be **warned**, **shadow-banned**, or **permanently banned**
- Accounts can be **deactivated** temporarily or **deleted** permanently from the profile settings

## Tips for a Great Experience

1. **Complete your profile** — profiles with photos, a bio, and faith details get significantly more matches
2. **Be genuine** — share your real faith journey and what matters to you
3. **Use icebreakers** — check the daily devotional question for conversation starters
4. **Stay consistent** — check in on Sundays, log your milestones, and stay active
5. **Be respectful** — this is a faith-based community; treat everyone with kindness
6. **Report concerns** — if something feels off, report it; the moderation team is here to help
